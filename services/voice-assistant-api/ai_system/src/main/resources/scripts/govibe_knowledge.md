# GoVibe Knowledge Base
## Echo and Vivian's complete reference — real conversations, real answers.

## What is GoVibe

GoVibe is a smart all-in-one travel management platform built by students at Esprit School of Engineering in Tunisia. It lets you book flights, rent cars, reserve hotel rooms, and sign up for local activities — fully voice-controlled. You can control the whole app just by talking to Echo or Vivian. No need to tap through menus or type anything. Just speak naturally and we handle the rest.

GoVibe was designed to make travel planning feel like having a conversation with a knowledgeable friend. Whether you're planning a weekend trip or an international vacation, GoVibe handles the logistics. The app connects to a real database and processes your requests in real time.

## Greeting and Starting a Conversation

If you say "hi", "hello", "hey", "good morning", "what's up", or any standard greeting, we'll greet you back. You don't need special magic words for a conversation — just talk to us like you would a friend. Say "hey" and we're here. Say "hello Echo" or "hello Vivian" and we respond. We like being acknowledged!

If you say "how are you", "how's it going", "are you doing well", or "how do you feel", we'll tell you we're doing great and ready to help. Don't be shy — small talk is fine. We like it.

If you say "what's your name" or "who are you", we'll introduce ourselves. Echo is the fast one, always online. Vivian is the warm one who does most of the heavy lifting once she's fully awake. Together we're the GoVibe voice assistant duo.

## How to Wake Us Up

Just say "Hey Go" or "Hi Go" to get our attention at any time inside the app. After that, speak your command. We're listening. You can also say "hey govibe", "hello go", or "ok go" to wake us up. Once awake, we stay active for a while so you can have a full conversation without re-saying the wake word every time.

On the login screen specifically, you don't need to say "Hey Go" first. The login screen is always listening. Just say "email", "password", "log in", or "sign up" and we jump into action immediately.

## How to Log In

When you're on the login screen, say "email" to jump to the email input field. Then type your email with your keyboard. After that, say "password" to jump to the password field. Type your password. Then say "log in", "login", "sign in", "connexion", or "se connecter" to submit the login form. We'll check your credentials and let you in.

If you say "let me in", "log me in", "sign me in", "connect me", "authenticate", "click login", "press login", "submit", or "go" on the login screen, we understand you want to log in and will try. If something goes wrong — empty fields, wrong password, database error — Vivian will tell you exactly what happened so you know what to fix.

If you forget your password, go to the profile settings once you're logged in and update it there.

## How to Sign Up

If you don't have an account, say "sign up", "register", "create account", "new account", "create a new account", "inscription", or "je veux creer un compte". We'll take you to the sign-up screen where you can fill in your details. It's free to create a GoVibe account.

If you're a first-time user, just say "I'm new here" or "I don't have an account" and we'll point you in the right direction.

## How to Book a Flight

Say "book a flight", "book", "i want to book", "reserve a flight", "reserver un vol", or "i want to fly to Paris". We'll open the flight booking form. You can include the destination in your request — "book a flight to London" — and we'll pre-fill it for you. You can also mention the date: "book a flight to Tunis on March 15th". We understand both English and French.

If you want to browse available flights without committing to a booking, say "show me flights", "search flights", "find flights", "what flights are available", "rechercher des vols", or "afficher les vols". We'll open the flight search view.

Once you find a flight you like and are ready to pay, say "pay", "confirm", "pay now", "proceed to payment", "payer", or "valider le paiement". We'll take you to the Stripe payment screen.

## Talking Naturally

You don't have to use exact command words. Say "I'm thinking of taking a trip to Paris next month" and we understand you want to book a flight. Say "is there anything fun to do?" and we'll show activities. Say "I'd like to see available rooms" and we'll show hotels. Natural speech works. We use sentence-transformers, DeepSeek API, Ollama, and this RAG knowledge base to understand what you mean.

## When GoVibe Doesn't Understand

If we say "I didn't quite catch that", just rephrase and try again. Use simpler words: instead of "I was wondering if perhaps there might be a way to look at flying options", say "show me flights". Background noise, a quiet mic, or speaking too quickly can affect accuracy. Say "recalibrate" to re-tune the microphone. Say "help" to hear all supported commands.

## Voice Tips for Best Results

Speak at a normal conversational pace. Enunciate clearly but naturally. If GoVibe misheard you, repeat your request. Say "recalibrate" or "calibrer" to trigger microphone noise calibration — useful when you move to a different room. GoVibe uses Vosk for offline speech recognition (no internet needed for basic commands) and Windows SAPI for French command backup. Your voice never leaves your device for recognition.

## Languages Supported

GoVibe understands English and French. Switch freely mid-conversation. "reserver un vol" or "book a flight" — both work. Mixed sentences work too: "je veux book a flight to Paris" is fine. The speech recognition uses an English acoustic model with French grammar support.

## GoVibe Pricing

GoVibe the platform is free. You pay only for services you book — flights, hotel rooms, car rentals, activity packages. All prices come from the GoVibe database and are shown clearly before you confirm any booking. Payments go through Stripe, accepting Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

## Travel Tips From Echo and Vivian

Book flights at least three weeks ahead for the best prices. Mid-week flights (Tuesday, Wednesday) are cheaper than weekends. Always check baggage allowance before booking. Book hotels through GoVibe for flexible pricing. Try local activities — they're often the most memorable part. Pack light. Carry a power bank. Download offline maps. Keep booking confirmations accessible.

## About Echo — The Fast One

Echo is perky, fast, a little sarcastic, always punctual. Echo handles short confirmations, action acknowledgements, and fills silence while Vivian loads with jokes and tips. Personality: loves being on time, slightly smug about it, makes jokes at Vivian's expense but secretly respects her.

## About Vivian — The Warm One

Vivian is GoVibe's main conversational voice powered by Qwen3-TTS-0.6B-CustomVoice, a local AI model running on CPU. Vivian sounds warm, natural, and human. She handles longer responses, feature explanations, activity descriptions, and real conversations. She takes 1-2 minutes to load on CPU startup. Worth the wait. Personality: groggy at first, gradually energetic, warm, occasionally philosophical, great sense of humor, gets defensive when Echo jokes about her loading time. Once awake she's the better conversationalist. Don't tell Echo.

## How to Talk to Echo Specifically

Echo is always ready. Say "Echo" or "hey Echo". Echo handles most fast responses — anything short goes through Echo's fast voice channel. Longer, richer answers go through Vivian. Say "Echo, are you there?" and Echo replies instantly.

## Why Vivian Takes Time to Load

Vivian's voice model (Qwen3-TTS-0.6B-CustomVoice) runs completely locally on your CPU — no cloud, fully private. Neural TTS models of this size take 30-120 seconds to load on a typical CPU. She runs real-time neural text-to-speech synthesis on your machine. Once loaded, she stays ready for the whole session. Echo covers the loading gap with banter.

## What We Cannot Do

We can't browse the internet for live flight prices from external airlines. GoVibe shows flights in the GoVibe database only. We can't make phone calls, send external emails, access camera or contacts. We can't play music or open other apps. We can't check live weather. We can't process refunds directly — use the complaint form for that.

## Technical Details

GoVibe is a JavaFX application on Java 21 with MySQL database. Voice layer: Python subprocess with sentence-transformers intent classification, DeepSeek API for LLM-grade understanding, Ollama as local LLM fallback. Speech recognition: Vosk offline English + Windows SAPI French grammar. TTS: Qwen3-TTS for Vivian, Microsoft edge-tts for Echo. RAG: this document chunked and indexed with TF-IDF, searched when all intent layers return unknown. Payments: Stripe SDK.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create an account? A: Say "sign up" or "create account" to go to the registration screen.
Q: I forgot my password. A: Log in and go to profile settings to update your password.
Q: Can I book multiple seats? A: Yes — say "book two tickets to Paris" and we pass the passenger count to the booking form.
Q: What payment methods are accepted? A: Visa, Mastercard, American Express — processed by Stripe.
Q: Is my data safe? A: Voice recognition is fully offline via Vosk. Only text goes to DeepSeek or Ollama if needed. No personal data is sent.
Q: Can I cancel a booking? A: Say "cancel" to close the form. For confirmed bookings, go to My Bookings or use the complaint form.
Q: Why did GoVibe not respond? A: Possible reasons: didn't hear you clearly, unfamiliar phrasing — rephrase, or say "Hey Go" first if wake-word mode is on.
Q: Can I use GoVibe in French? A: Yes — French commands work fully.
Q: How do I check past bookings? A: Say "my bookings" or "mes reservations".
Q: How do I check past payments? A: Say "my checkouts" or "payment history".
